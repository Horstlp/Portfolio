About Me

My name is Kevin Jeitler, and I am 16 years old. I am currently attending HTL Spengergasse, where I am pursuing studies in game design. My passion for creating immersive and engaging virtual worlds has driven me to this field, and I am excited to develop my skills and knowledge in game development. At HTL Spengergasse, I am learning various aspects of game design, from conceptualizing ideas and storytelling to mastering the technical skills needed for game programming and graphic design. I am eager to contribute to the future of the gaming industry and look forward to creating games that captivate and inspire players worldwide.




My School

The School I go to is the HTL Spengergasse it is a higher technical school offering a wide range of educational and training opportunities for both young people and adults. With a long-standing tradition in vocational education, we collaborate closely with industry and are committed to innovative thinking, contemporary teamwork, and project-based teaching methods. As a school that prepares students for life, our foremost priority is to provide comprehensive professional education and training, along with fostering social competence in our students.
